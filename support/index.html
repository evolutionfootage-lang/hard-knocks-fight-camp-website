Support

Need help using Hard Knocks Fight Camp? Check the information below or contact us directly.

Basic Troubleshooting

If the app is not working as expected, try closing and reopening it, checking that your device volume is turned up and confirming that your device is running the latest available version of iOS.

You can also check the App Store for an available update to Hard Knocks Fight Camp.

Audio Issues

If you cannot hear workout instructions or audio call-outs, check your device volume, silent mode and any connected Bluetooth headphones or speakers.

Reporting a Problem

When reporting an issue, please include your iPhone model, iOS version, app version and a brief description of what happened.

Contact Support

Email: support@hardknocksfightcamp.com

We aim to respond to support requests as soon as reasonably possible.
