Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 July 2026

Hard Knocks Fight Camp respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Hard Knocks Fight Camp mobile application.

Information We Collect

Hard Knocks Fight Camp does not require users to create an account and does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number or physical address through the app.

Workout Information

Information such as workout selections, preferences or completed sessions may be stored locally on your device so that the app can operate correctly and remember your settings.

Locally stored information is not transmitted to Hard Knocks Fight Camp unless this is clearly explained to you within the app.

Health Information

The app provides general fitness and kickboxing training content. It does not diagnose medical conditions or provide medical advice.

Hard Knocks Fight Camp does not access Apple Health data unless a future app feature clearly requests your permission before doing so.

Analytics and Tracking

The current version of Hard Knocks Fight Camp does not use third-party advertising trackers and does not track users across apps or websites owned by other companies.

Advertising

Hard Knocks Fight Camp does not display third-party advertising.

Purchases

Any purchases made through the app are processed by Apple through the App Store. Hard Knocks Fight Camp does not receive or store your payment card information.

Children’s Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information associated with the app. However, no electronic storage or transmission method can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when the app or its features change. The updated version will be published on this page with a revised date.
